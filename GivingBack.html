Giving back to the society will go a long way!

I believe, good education is every child's right. But it's no brainer that we are far from this being a reality. What can we do? One easy way to extend a helping hand those who have taken this responsibility on their shoulders. While some organizations are taking big steps to make a difference, we can definitely help in small ways. Just spreading the awareness, volunteering once in a while or donating small bits can go a long way in creating an impact. Feel free to browse through some of the leading organizations in different parts of the world, I have listed a few. I emphasize on education and primary healthcare, so I picked those organizations. Small steps today will create a big impact tomorrow :)

UNICEF

UNICEF is committed to ensuring that all children – regardless of gender, ethnicity, socioeconomic background or circumstance – realise their right to a quality education.

Child Rights and You (CRY)

Child Rights and You (CRY) works towards restoring basic rights to children and works across levels from direct action to advocacy, mobilizing public opinion and policy change.

Make a Difference (MAD)

Make a Difference works with nearly 5000 children in 77 shelter homes across 23 cities in India, delivering impact through a Fellow-managed volunteering model of 4000 young leaders.

Barefoot College

Barefoot College is a voluntary organisation working in the fields of education, skill development, health, drinking water, women empowerment and electrification through solar power for the upliftment of rural people

CARE India

CARE has been working in India for over 65 years, focusing on alleviating poverty and social injustice through well planned and comprehensive programmes in health, education, livelihoods and disaster preparedness and response.

Smile Foundation

Smile India Foundation directly implements development projects for underprivileged communities in villages as well as urban slums across the country focusing on family health, livelihood and women empowerment.

Asha (Hope) for Education

Asha for Education is a fully volunteer-run non-profit running over 200 projects across India, focusing on basic education to underprivileged children in India, healthcare, environment and women empowerment.
